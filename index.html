What is Doskort and why should you be using it?

Doskort is a social networking app made for sharing photos and videos from a smartphone. Similar to Facebook or Twitter, everyone who creates an Doskort account has a profile and a news feed.

When you post a photo or video on Doskort, it will be displayed on your profile. Other users who follow you will see your posts in their own feed. Likewise, you'll see posts from other users whom you choose to follow.

Pretty straight forward, right? It's like a simplified version of Facebook, with an emphasis on mobile use and visual sharing. Just like other social networks, you can interact with other users on Doskotr by following them, being followed by them, commenting, liking, tagging and private messaging. You can even save the photos you see on Doskort.
